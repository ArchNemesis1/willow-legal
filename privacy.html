Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 06/18/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Willow! This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we share it. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

A. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (for email sign-in) or your Google profile information (for Google Sign-In). We also store the username and profile picture you choose for your Willow profile.
  • Pet & Activity Information: We collect all the data you create about your pets, including their names, breeds, birthdates, notes, appointments, medical documents, and reminders.
  • Forum Content: We collect the text and images you post in the community forums, as well as any comments or replies you make.

B. Information Collected Automatically:

  • Push Notification Token: To send you notifications for reminders and forum activity, we collect and store a unique push notification token for your device, provided by Expo's services.
  • Usage Data: Our backend service (Google Firebase) may automatically collect standard log information, including crash data and performance metrics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the core functionality of the Willow app.
  • To manage your account and user profile.
  • To display your content (pet profiles, forum posts) to other users as intended by the service.
  • To send you scheduled reminders and forum-related push notifications.
  • To perform automated content moderation on forum posts and images to detect and flag potentially inappropriate content.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.

4. How We Share Your Information

Your data is shared only with the essential third-party services that power the app. We do not sell your personal data.

  • Google (Firebase & Google Cloud): We use Google's Firebase platform as our backend. All of your data (user profiles, pet information, forum content, etc.) is stored on Google's Firestore and Cloud Storage. We also use Google Cloud Vision for image moderation.
  • Expo: We use Expo's services to build the app and to handle the delivery of push notifications.

5. Data Security

We rely on the robust security measures provided by Google Cloud and Firebase to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this section of the app.
